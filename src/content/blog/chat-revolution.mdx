Traditional business interfaces often feel like walking through a maze of menus, buttons, and forms. But what if interacting with your business systems was as natural as having a conversation?

```

│  and share with the team"      │
```

This simplification isn't just about convenience – it's about fundamentally changing how we interact with business systems. Mark, an e-commerce store owner, reduced his team's training time by 80% after implementing a chat-based interface. "Instead of learning complex systems," he says, "they just ask for what they need."
